Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

TL;DR Version

We only collect data to fix your car, keep you updated via WhatsApp, and make our site run faster. We don’t sell your data to third parties because that’s cringe. Your machine’s specs and your contact info are safe with us.

01. Data Collection

When you book a service, we collect your Name, Phone Number, and Vehicle Details. This is strictly used to schedule your appointment and ensure our master techs have the right parts ready for your specific model.

02. WhatsApp & Communication

By using our booking portal, you agree to be redirected to WhatsApp. We use this platform for instant status updates, repair photos, and service confirmations. You can opt-out at any time by simply muting or blocking the chat.

03. Cookies & Tech

We use minimal cookies to remember your preferences (like if you prefer Cars or Bikes). This helps the site load faster the next time you visit. No "creepy" tracking involved.

04. Your Rights

You own your data. If you want us to wipe your service history or contact info from our records, shoot us an email at privacy@revlab.com and we’ll handle it immediately.
